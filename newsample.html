Concert and Sports Center
in Angeles City, Pampanga

The province of Pampanga, specifically Angeles City, may soon become a prime destination for concerts and sports events with the upcoming opening of a new convention center.

The new facility, funded by Senator Lito Lapid, is located in Barangay Pampang of said city.

Yna Ramos Reynoso, president of RC Ramos Construction and the project’s contractor, said the facility has a 5,000-seat capacity and meets the standards for both national and international sports events.

The arena, with hundreds of parking slots, can accommodate a large number of guests, she added.

Reynoso said the arena is expected to be opened by February next year.

“It will be an ideal venue for sports events, musical concerts, and community gatherings for Angeleños and Kapampangans,” she said.
